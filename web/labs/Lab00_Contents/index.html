Dat Boi

About the Meme

Dat Boi, a colloquial pronunciation of “that boy,” is a nickname given to a 3D character model of a green frog riding a unicycle, typically accompanied by phrases such as “here comes dat boi!” and “o [redacted] waddup,” which became a viral meme on social media in early 2016.
